Scope

The goal of this project is to prove how photography using our mobile devices has increased since the evolution of smartphone cameras which are improving year by year, thus reducing the amount of traditional camera sales and usage.

Constraints

There are only a few main constraints for this hypothesis. Mainly as a matter of fact the current smartphones are now able to capture as much details as DSLR both in video and photos due to the smaller size of the sensor present in the smartphone cameras. And this may lead some consumers to opt to buy a mirrorless or a DSLR camera

research

After many hours of research and reading through one article to another I came to conclusion which helped draw my hypothesis which explains how smartphones are capturing more pictures every day when compared to a traditional camera In order to validate my hypothesis, I have sourced information from various websites. The trend of smartphone photography has increased due to selfie photography and the compact and ease of use of smartphones
